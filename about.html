About QR Scanner

Our QR Scanner project is designed to provide a fast, secure, and user-friendly experience for scanning QR codes using your camera or uploaded images. Built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, it’s fully browser-based, responsive, and modern.

Features

Instant Scanning

Scan QR codes in milliseconds with high accuracy using advanced browser technology.

Secure & Private

No data is stored or shared. Your scans remain completely private and secure.

Mobile Friendly

Works seamlessly on all devices including smartphones, tablets, and desktops.

User Friendly

Easy to use interface that allows everyone to scan QR codes without complications.
